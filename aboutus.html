Welcome to
Akte Dessert

Akte Dessert was founded by Amou Roux in 1964. At age 7, he started to learn dessert making. In order to promote the dessert of his homeland, British, Amou opened a restaurant located in downtown Paris. The origin of the brand name comes from Akte, who is the goddess of dining in Greek mythology. Amou considered her as a synonym for delicacy and represented as an attraction to the customers.

Nowadays, Akte Dessert mainly focuses on Europe's and East Asian's market, We desire to develop the market in North America in the future.

Our restaurant provided a comfortable environment for customers to relax, to taste and to share their experiences about dessert.We served a series of traditional English dessert and homemade dessert to the customers worldwide. The color scheme of our product included pink, brown and white, so that our customer can enjoy the dessert with an easy mind.

Our target customers are worker, student, housewife and anyone who love our desserts.
